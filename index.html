Our Story

Serving Authentic Flavors for Over 30 Years

Since 1993, we have been the premier destination for authentic cuisine that tantalizes the taste buds. Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest ingredients to create unforgettable dining experiences.

From our succulent kebabs and tandooris to our wood-fired pizzas, aromatic biryanis, rich curries, and authentic South Indian dishes, every dish tells a story of culinary excellence and passion.

We take pride in our warm, inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a beloved institution in the culinary world.

Our Specialties

Authentic Kebabs

Succulent, tender kebabs marinated in traditional spices and grilled to perfection over open flames.

Sizzling Tandooris

Flavorful meats and vegetables cooked in our traditional clay tandoor, infused with smoky aromas.

Wood-Fired Pizzas

Crispy, thin-crust pizzas baked in our traditional wood-fired ovens with the finest ingredients.

Aromatic Biryanis

Fragrant basmati rice layered with tender meat and exotic spices, cooked using age-old techniques.

Rich Curries

Hearty, flavorful curries simmered slowly to develop deep, complex flavors that delight the senses.

South Indian Delicacies

Authentic dosas, idlis, vadas, and uttapams served with traditional chutneys and sambar.
